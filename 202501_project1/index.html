Glossary

Director
The person responsible for the creative vision of a movie.
Cinematography
The art of capturing visual images on film.
Genre
A category of movies based on style or subject matter.
Plot
The main story of a movie.
Sequel
A movie that continues the story of a previous movie.
Prequel
A movie that tells the story that happened before a previous movie.
Starring
The actors who play roles in the movie.
Soundtrack
The music that accompanies a movie.
Trailer
A short preview of a movie.
Box office
The total amount of money made from the sale of tickets for a movie.
Rating
A score given to a movie to indicate its quality.
